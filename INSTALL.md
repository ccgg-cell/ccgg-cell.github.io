# UPDATE 3 of 4 — Live Civil War News Carousel
**Deploy: Day 7-10 of testing window**
**Version bump: 1.2.0 → 1.3.0**

## What this adds
An AI-powered news carousel that searches the web for real Civil War and relic hunting news.
Updates every 6 hours automatically. NC stories prioritized.
Appears above the footer section of the app.

## How to install (2 steps)

### Step 1 — Upload to GitHub
Upload: update-3-news.js
Commit: "v1.3 - Add live Civil War news carousel"

### Step 2 — Add to index.html
Just before </body> add:

    <script src="update-3-news.js"></script>

Commit: "v1.3 - Wire news carousel"

### Step 3 — Bump version in Play Console
Version name: 1.3.0
